Food Delivery App Customer Support Policy
1. Purpose

This Customer Support Policy defines how customer issues, complaints, inquiries, and feedback are handled within the Food Delivery Application. The objective is to provide timely, professional, and effective assistance to customers, restaurant partners, and delivery personnel.

2. Support Channels

Customers can contact support through:

In-App Chat Support
Email Support
Help Center / FAQ
Contact Form
Phone Support (if available)
Support Availability
Channel	Availability
In-App Chat	24/7
Email Support	24/7
Phone Support	Business Hours
FAQ Portal	24/7
3. Response Time Commitment
Priority	Example Issue	Response Time
Critical	Payment deducted but order not placed	Within 1 hour
High	Order not delivered	Within 2 hours
Medium	Wrong item received	Within 24 hours
Low	General inquiries	Within 48 hours
4. Order Related Support

Customers may request assistance for:

Order Not Delivered

Support team will:

Verify order status.
Contact delivery partner.
Provide delivery update.
Issue refund if required.
Wrong Item Delivered

Customers should:

Report within 24 hours.
Upload supporting photos.

Resolution options:

Refund
Replacement
Store Credit
Missing Items

Support will:

Verify restaurant order details.
Investigate delivery records.
Process partial refund when applicable.
5. Payment Support

Supported issues include:

Payment failure
Duplicate payment
Refund status
Wallet balance issues

Refund timelines:

Payment Method	Refund Time
Credit Card	5–10 Business Days
Debit Card	5–10 Business Days
UPI	1–5 Business Days
Wallet	24 Hours
6. Cancellation Policy Support
Customer Cancellation

Customers may cancel orders before restaurant preparation begins.

Restaurant Cancellation

If a restaurant cancels:

Full refund will be initiated.
Promotional credits may be issued.
Delivery Partner Cancellation

Alternative delivery assignment will be attempted before cancellation.

7. Refund Policy

Refunds may be approved for:

Non-delivery
Incorrect order
Damaged food
Duplicate payment
Technical system failures

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the order.

8. Customer Conduct

Customers are expected to:

Provide accurate information.
Communicate respectfully.
Avoid fraudulent refund claims.
Refrain from abusive language toward support agents.

Violation may result in:

Warning
Temporary suspension
Permanent account termination
